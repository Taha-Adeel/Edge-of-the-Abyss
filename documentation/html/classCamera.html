Detailed Description

Camera class is wrapper for sf::view.

This will just take care of what region to be seen on the window. you can scroll, rotate or zoom the entire scene without altering the way that your drawable objects are drawn.

Update the position of camera.

In x_direction : it will check whether the distance b/w x coordinates (of top left corner) of player and camera is greater than CAMERA_OFFSET_X_LEFT, if it is, then update the position of camera.
